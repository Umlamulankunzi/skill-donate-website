About Us

Skill Donate is a platform where volunteers can offer their skills as donations to non-profits, or show interest and take up skills posted by non-profits as required.

The website also provides a platform for nonprofits to post skills they require and to browse skills offered by volunteers posted on the website.

Volunteers

Volunteers who wish to engage with Charity Organizations by donating their skills and time can use the Skill Donate platform to register a simple account and start offering their skills for donation. They can also offer to donate to skill requirements posted by Charity Organizations.

As a Volunteer, you can:

  • Create an account: Sign up as a volunteer on Skill Donate by providing your personal information and creating a secure login.
  • Browse Skill Requirements: As a volunteer, you can browse through the skill requirements posted by non-profit organizations.
  • Post Skill Donations: Also, as a volunteers can post the specific skills they would like to donate on the platform and wait for Charity Organizations to show interest in the donated skills.
  • Sharing experiences: Volunteers can also post testimonials on the platform, sharing their experiences of working with Charity Organisations.

Charity Organizations

Charity Organizations who are seeking donations in the form of skills from skilled volunteers can register on the platform and connect with volunteers wishing to donate their skills. Charity Organizations can also browse donated skills posted by volunteers organizations.

As a Charity Organization, you can:

  • Create an account: Charity organizations can register on the Skill Donate platform to access the full features and functionalities.
  • Browse Donated Skills: Browse the skills posted by volunteers and select the ones they require for their projects or initiatives.
  • Post Skill Requirements: Alternatively, Charity Organizations can post the specific skills they require on the platform and wait for volunteers to show interest and offer their skills.
  • Sharing experiences: Charity Organizations can also post testimonials on the platform, sharing their experiences of working with skilled volunteers.
